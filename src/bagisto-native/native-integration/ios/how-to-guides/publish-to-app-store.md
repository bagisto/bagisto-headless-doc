# How to Live iOS App

Taking your app "live" involves submitting it to Apple for review via App Store Connect.

## Step 1: App Store Connect
1. Visit [appstoreconnect.apple.com](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/).
2. Create a **New App** record.
3. Fill in the metadata, screenshots, and descriptions.

## Step 2: Distribute Archive
1. Open the **Organizer** in Xcode (**Window > Organizer**).
2. Select your latest archive.
3. Click **Distribute App**.
4. Select **App Store Connect** and follow the prompts to upload your build.

## Step 3: TestFlight (Optional but Recommended)
1. Once the build is uploaded, go to the **TestFlight** tab in App Store Connect.
2. Add internal or external testers to verify the app before public release.

## Step 4: Submit for Review
1. Go to the **App Store** tab.
2. Select the build you uploaded.
3. Click **Add for Review**.
4. Wait for Apple's feedback (usually 24-48 hours).
